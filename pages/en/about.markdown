_Polyclinic Jelovac specialises in **early diagnostics** and **prevention** of malignant diseases. State-of-the-art medical equipment, years of experience and continuous education allow us to provide the best diagnostics and successful prevention of malignant diseases._


Years of experience, continuous education and state-of-the-art medical equipment enable the best diagnostics and prevention of malignant diseases.

Since 1991 we have refined and equipped ourselves to the point where we can detect very small tumour formations (from as little as 3 mm). Detected formations are punctured under ultrasound guidance in order to obtain a tissue sample for cytological (microscopic) diagnosis. This is the procedure by which we establish the condition and enable treatment of the disease at its earliest stage, when treatment outcomes are mostly successful (cure through minor surgery without additional radiation or chemotherapy).

With Colour Doppler diagnostics we detect vascular diseases at an early stage when treatment is possible and prevent the development of complications.

## Systematic Check-ups

A timely diagnosis provides the opportunity for quality, rapid treatment and recovery without major trauma to the body. We therefore recommend preventive systematic check-ups every year, even if you have no complaints.

A basic systematic check-up includes: clinical examination, ECG, laboratory diagnostics, abdominal ultrasound, breast ultrasound and other examinations, depending on the agreement with the patient.
